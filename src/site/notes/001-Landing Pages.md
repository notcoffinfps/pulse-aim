Welcome to pulseAim, a repository for aiming focused articles and blogs mainly about improvements and the intricacy of aiming. pulseAim is a passion project that aims to provide access to high quality resources compared to the quantity of resources, our goal is to help players understand and accelerate their aiming improvement. There is no set schedule for when an article or blog will be published, but people in the Discord server (soon) will be notified when a new one is published.

This project is highly inspired by RiddBTW, the legendary improvement-focused aim content creator. Ridd has inspired me in almost every aspect of aiming and learning in general, his approach to producing easy-to-follow learning material is the blueprint of this whole project. The lack of aiming comprehension seen in the general public is also the main driving force behind creating this project. I want to create mainly research-based resources and what I've learned along my aim progression. Besides that, I will also talk about proper techniques instead of fabricating theoretical nonsense or posting something for the sole purpose of being a contrarian. My peak rank is Astra on 
VT Aimlabs S2, I have been in several aiming groups previously such as Ultraviolet and Astrum, I've also worked with high-tier aim coach and aimer in general such as minigod, Cartoon, RobbFPS, sillydomi, and Skylr to name a few, I am currently also the lead resource team and aimer on IATC. With that being said, I believe I am credible enough to share knowledge and tips on aiming.
